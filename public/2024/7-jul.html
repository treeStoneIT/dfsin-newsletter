Leave a Great Lasting Impression

We've all heard the old cliché, "Don't judge a book by its cover," but we do!

Many people make important judgments, right or wrong, on first impressions. Because most prospects will judge you in the first two minutes of your initial contact, you must look the part, walk the part, and talk the part.

These simple tips will help you make a good first impression and leave a great lasting impression:

  • Pay attention to the way you shake hands.
  • Make good eye contact.
  • Be professional in how you dress.
  • Focus your attention on the other person.
  • Be the first to speak and introduce yourself.
  • Be sincere and willing to listen.
  • Be positive and enthusiastic.
  • On the telephone, your first words are crucial. Follow a script that you have memorized, professionalized and personalized!

Make a good first impression -- leave a great lasting impression!
