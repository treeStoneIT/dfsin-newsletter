Be Courageous

“One man with courage makes a majority.”- Andrew Jackson

After Dr. David Livingstone had begun his now famous missionary work in Africa, a certain group wrote the great pioneer and asked: "Have you found a good road to where you are? If so, please advise. We'd like to send others to assist you."

Dr. Livingstone sent this reply: "If you have those who will come only if they know there is a good road, I don't want them. I want individuals who are strong and courageous – those who will come if there is no road at all!"

What a lesson in courage! Courage is performing the task that would be easier not to do. Courage is accepting the responsibilities when it would be more comfortable not to accept. It's moving out on faith, when you know full well the risks and the pitfalls that will challenge you. It's marching ahead, blazing a new trail where there are no roads, and sometimes no maps to guide you.

So make one more call. Keep going! Courage will help you make the sale.

Good luck and good selling!

Ted Talk

Intelligent Advisor advice is at the forefront of what we do now. We’re no longer ‘sales people’ who need to sell the product. According to LIMRA, 82% of people use the internet during the life insurance buying process.

Almost half will research online and buy in person. Only 13% will research and buy online. What that tells us is that most consumers are knowledgeable about the products we sell, but to a point where they want to sit with an advisor.

They want the validation of knowing what they’ve researched online to be true, so they will still buy from an advisor. Clients prefer face-to-face over buying over phone, mail or video service. This is the trust age. We’re there team. Trust is paramount.
